1. Introduction

Welcome to SuperYou ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when using our App:

  • Profile Information: Name, age, gender, height, weight
  • Health Information: Activity level, health conditions (e.g., diabetes, IBS, celiac), allergies, pregnancy/breastfeeding status, dietary preferences
  • Meal & Nutrition Data: Food logs, meal history, nutrient intake, dietary habits
  • Goals: Weight goals, dietary goals, lifestyle factors (smoking, alcohol, caffeine consumption)

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Screens viewed, features used, app interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Analytics: App open events, food entry events (non-personal identifiers)

2.3 Payment Information

  • Premium subscription status (active/inactive)
  • Purchase history is processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not store your credit card or payment details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized nutrition recommendations and meal suggestions
  • Calculate your daily nutrient requirements (DRI) based on your profile
  • Track your meals and analyze nutritional intake
  • Estimate blood sugar impact based on your health conditions
  • Identify nutrient deficiencies and provide educational warnings
  • Improve our app features and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Process premium subscriptions (via Apple App Store)

4. Health Information Notice & Consent

Important: This App is for informational purposes only and is not a medical device. Health information provided (such as blood sugar estimates and nutrient deficiency warnings) is for educational purposes and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before making health decisions.

Explicit Consent for Health Data

By using this App, you explicitly consent to the collection and processing of your health-related information, including but not limited to:

  • Health conditions (diabetes, IBS, celiac, etc.)
  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding status
  • Allergies and dietary restrictions
  • Activity level and lifestyle factors
  • Meal and nutrition data

This data is processed solely to provide personalized nutrition recommendations and health insights within the App. You may withdraw this consent at any time by deleting your data or uninstalling the App.

We collect health information to:

  • Personalize your nutrition recommendations
  • Adjust calculations for specific health conditions (e.g., diabetes)
  • Provide relevant nutritional guidance

We do NOT:

  • Share your health information with third parties for advertising
  • Sell your health data

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (ObjectBox database)
  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account or sign in
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly export it
  • Encryption: We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Service Providers: Apple (for App Store purchases and analytics via Firebase Analytics with anonymized data)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: If you explicitly request data export or sharing

Third-Party Services:

  • Apple App Store: Processes premium subscription payments
  • Firebase Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics (no personally identifiable information)

7. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and Other Privacy Laws)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete: Request deletion of all your data (available in Settings > Legal > Delete All My Data)
  • Export: Export your nutrition history data (available in app)
  • Correct: Update your profile information at any time in Settings

To exercise these rights, use the in-app features or contact us at: contact@superyou.life

8. Data Retention

  • Your data is stored on your device until you delete it

  • Premium status information is retained by Apple per their retention policy

9. Children's Privacy & Adult Supervision

Our App is not intended for children under 18 without adult supervision. Users between 13-17 years of age must use this App under the supervision and guidance of a parent or legal guardian.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately for deletion.

Adult Supervision Required For:
